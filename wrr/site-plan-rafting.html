Overview

Purpose

This rafting website aims to attract adventure seekers and will provide safety procedures and rafting trips.

Audience

Our target audience consists of individuals and groups ages 18-50 years old who are interested in adventures.

Style Guide

Color Palette

We have chosen a vibrant color palette consisting of blue (#396e94), orange (#e7c24f), red (#a43312), dark purple (#381d2a), and light gray (#aabd8c). This combination evokes a sense of adventure, excitement, and energy, reflecting the nature of the rafting experience and appealing to our target audience of young adults and adventure enthusiasts.

Typography

Use actual font names or font families for headings, normal text, and colored callout text.
